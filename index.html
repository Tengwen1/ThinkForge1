Founder System

Founder-led governance with AI assistance

ThinkForge is not designed as a random crowd project. It is designed with a clear chain of review: users build, AI evaluates, and the founder makes the final call.

How decisions work

1
Users submit ideas and projects
Anyone can participate and cooperate using separate accounts.
2
Multiple AI judges score the work
Logic, creativity, usefulness, difficulty, technical quality, and impact all matter.
3
Founder reviews the results
AI can assist, but it does not own the final decision.
4
Winners are featured or advanced
Great work can be highlighted, recognized, or developed into a real startup path.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Important questions about the platform, company, and founder system.

What is ThinkForge?

ThinkForge is a future-focused technology company and platform for thinking, building, and launching. It aims to connect software, hardware, AI systems, and startup culture.

Who decides winners?

Multiple AI judges can help evaluate submissions, but the final decision belongs to the founder.

Can users cooperate together?

Yes. Users can work together from separate accounts instead of needing one fixed shared group account.

Is ThinkForge only software?

No. The long-term vision includes software, hardware, and AI chips.

Are prizes common?

No. Prizes are meant for rare occasions or holidays. Most rewards are ranking, visibility, featured placement, and reputation.
